## Core Reframing

The goal is not just to learn neuroscience facts.

The real goal is to understand how mathematical and computational models can explain neural information processing, behavior, cognition, and their possible connections to modern AI systems.

## Key Questions

## Current Working Hypothesis

Computational neuroscience can act as a bridge between:

- biological neural systems;
- artificial neural networks;
- cognitive models;
- behavior;
- human-AI interaction;
- external cognition systems.

However, this bridge requires careful separation between:

- strict mathematical equivalence;
- partial structural similarity;
- useful engineering analogy;
- speculative metaphor.
