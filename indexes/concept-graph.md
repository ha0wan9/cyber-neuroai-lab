## Link Types

- `requires`: prerequisite concept.
- `supports`: helps explain or test another concept.
- `bridges-to`: connects domains without implying identity.
- `contrasts-with`: clarifies a boundary or misconception.
- `experiment-for`: can be probed with a small runnable artifact.
- `paper-evidence-for`: supported or challenged by literature.
