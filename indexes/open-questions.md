## Question Types

- `concept`: clarifies a term, mechanism, or distinction.
- `experiment`: can be probed with code, simulation, or data analysis.
- `paper`: needs literature support or disagreement.
- `workflow`: concerns learning, review, memory, or agent use.
- `research`: may become a project direction.
- `public`: may become an essay, tutorial, or talk.

## Triage Rule

During review, each active question should move toward one of:

- a note;
- an experiment;
- a paper-reading target;
- a backlog item;
- a public writing candidate;
- an archived question with a short reason.
