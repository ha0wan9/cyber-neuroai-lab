## Status Labels

- `raw`: captured but not transformed.
- `structured`: converted into a note, map, or experiment plan.
- `tested`: linked to a runnable experiment or worked example.
- `reviewed`: checked during weekly or monthly consolidation.
- `public-candidate`: strong enough to become public writing.

## Update Rule

When a source changes understanding, add or update one linked artifact:

- concept note in `notes/`
- experiment in `experiments/`
- paper note in `papers/`
- question in `indexes/open-questions.md`
- backlog item in `indexes/research-backlog.md`
