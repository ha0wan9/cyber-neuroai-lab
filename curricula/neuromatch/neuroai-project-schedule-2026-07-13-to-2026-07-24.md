## Milestone Contracts

### W1D4 — Project Proposal

- one-sentence motivation;
- one selected direction and one explicit backup;
- approximately three questions, ordered from required to optional;
- a workflow diagram from source/code/data to analysis and interpretation;
- feasibility boundary: compute, data access, time, and stopping rule;
- division of work and immediate next action.

### W2D3 — Progress Report

- what ran successfully;
- what evidence exists so far;
- what failed or remains blocked;
- any deviation from the W1D4 plan and why;
- the smallest credible result that can still be delivered by W2D5.

### W2D5 — Final Presentation

- question and motivation;
- method and controls;
- results with uncertainty and failure cases;
- interpretation separated from speculation;
- limitations and the next experiment.
